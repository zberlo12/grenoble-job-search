# Job Search System

This system automatically finds job listings from your email alerts, tracks your applications in Notion, and helps you write tailored CVs and cover letters — all through a conversational AI assistant.

---

## Before you start

You need two things:

1. **Claude Code** — the AI assistant that runs this system
   Download it at [claude.ai/code](https://claude.ai/code)

2. **A free Notion account** — where your job search data is stored
   Sign up at [notion.com](https://www.notion.com)

---

## Setup

**1. Get the code**

Click the green **Code** button on this page and choose **Download ZIP**, then unzip it.
Or if you use Git: `git clone https://github.com/zberlo12/grenoble-job-search.git`

**2. Open the folder in Claude Code**

Open Claude Code, then open the folder you just downloaded.

**3. Type this command**

```
/job-user-setup
```

That's it. Claude will guide you through everything else — creating your Notion workspace,
filling in your profile, connecting your email, and scheduling your daily scan.
The whole process takes about 15–20 minutes.

---

## Switching between users

If more than one person uses this system on the same computer, type:
```
/job-user-select
```

---

## Updating your profile

To change your salary target, add job titles, or update any other setting:
```
/job-user-profile
```
